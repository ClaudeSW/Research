04 — About
The Researcher
Your Photo

Lucas J. Fawcett

Syracuse University

I'm a student at Syracuse University researching the intersection of AI and engineering workflows. "Giving Claude Hands" investigates whether an AI language model can operate professional CAD software autonomously when given appropriate tooling and iterative feedback.

My interests include CAD/CAM automation, AI-assisted design, additive manufacturing, and aerospace engineering. This project demonstrates that with the right architecture, an AI can build real 3D geometry from natural language alone.
